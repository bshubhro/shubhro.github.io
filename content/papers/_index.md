## Working papers

- **Adapting for scale: Experimental evidence on computer-aided instruction in India**  
  _Working paper._  
  Experimental evidence on how computer-aided instruction performs when moved from tightly controlled pilots to larger-scale implementation, and what this implies for education policy and program design.

## Works in progress

- **Coordination frictions and last-mile delivery of public services**  
  _Concept note and pilot design (with Narendar Singh Bhandari)._  
  Reorganizing existing frontline health and nutrition workers across departments to improve immunization, antenatal care, and related outcomes at scale in India.

- **Historical institutions and political selection**  
  _Early-stage work in progress._  
  Linking colonial land tenure systems to modern political competition and the allocation of state capacity in India.

- **Local governance, bank branches, and financial inclusion**  
  _Data work and empirical design._  
  Using data on bank branch expansion and gram panchayat boundaries to study how local political jurisdictions and economies of scale shape access to formal financial services.
